Support Services

Helping Women Lead Cancer-Free Lives

At Yemanja Cancer Care Initiative, Inc., we care for the well-being of women in Nigeria. Breast cancer is one of the leading causes of death among women globally along with cervical cancer and HPV. Our goal is to combat this growing health concern among women through early prevention, diagnosis, and educational training.

Cervical Cancer Screening

We provide cancer screening to detect cervical cancer as early as possible. Our team and healthcare partners will conduct an early diagnosis and treatment approach for eligible women.

Breast Cancer Screening

We help women identify early signs of breast cancer through our cancer screening. Our programs aim to support women and save their lives against cancer.

Education and Training

Every woman in Nigeria deserves to get access and information about breast cancer, cervical cancer, and HPV. Our cancer care initiatives and programs provide training for local health workers and members of the community about Women cancer awareness.
