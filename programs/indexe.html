WCPI Programs and Outreach

Education and Outreach

Lack of health awareness and education are major factors behind high mortality rates from breast and cervical cancer.

WCPI collaborates with healthcare providers, faith-based groups, and community leaders to increase awareness with culturally appropriate messages, currently focused in Remo North, Ogun State.

Capacity Development

We offer training for local nurses and community health workers to improve screening and early detection.

Our collaboration with Project ECHO helps deliver shared cancer education and training for underserved areas in Nigeria.

Cancer Screening & Detection

We provide safe, effective, and free cervical cancer screening and treatment of pre-cancerous lesions in Remo North Local Government in Ogun State. It also comes with clinical breast examinations while teaching women how to conduct self-breast examinations regularly.

Our goal is to screen 80% of eligible women in the local government area within 3 years.

HPV Vaccination Advocacy

Vaccination of girls between the ages of 9 and 14 years is known to be effective, yet only a small proportion of girls in Nigeria have been vaccinated against this highly preventable cancer.

WCPI, in partnership with a local academic group, is advocating for HPV vaccination to contribute to the WHO's goals for the eradication of cervical cancer.
