Who We Are

Yemanja Cancer Care Initiative, an IRS 501(c)(3)-designated nonprofit organization founded in 2020, is dedicated to saving women’s lives through early breast cancer detection/diagnosis and treatment.

Our Approach

Screening and Early Diagnosis

In the first 2 years, utilizing our mobile mammography service, we will screen a cross-section of 70,000 women in the 6 Nigerian geopolitical zones. Participants will undergo repeat mammography every 1.5 to 2 years for a 6-year longitudinal study.

Immediate Benefit: Timely Treatment

Women diagnosed with breast cancer (after ultrasound and biopsy) will be referred to facilities for treatment based on international guidelines. We anticipate up to 300 women will need treatment during the project.

Long-Term Benefit: Risk Prediction

Data collected will be used to train deep learning algorithms for risk prediction from their initial screening mammogram — forming the basis for a cost-effective public health tool to reduce mortality.
