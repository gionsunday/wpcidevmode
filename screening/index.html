Empowering Women, One Screen at a Time

At the Women Cancer Prevention Initiative, we believe every woman deserves an easy, caring path to knowing her health status. Our screening programs bring together skilled nurses, friendly community health workers, and state-of-the-art equipment—right where you live—to make early detection simple and stress-free.

Our Screening Journey: From Clinic to Community

No woman should have to travel miles or pay high fees for a lifesaving checkup. That’s why WCPI’s outreach teams offer mammograms and cervical screenings in villages, markets, schools, and community centers across Nigeria. We treat every visit as an opportunity, not just to test, but to listen, support, and guide each woman through her health journey. We understand the challenges—long distances, busy schedules, and high costs. So we bring the screening to you– no referral, no stress, no payment needed.

Mobile Unit Success: Bringing Care to Your Doorstep

Our specially adapted screening van isn’t just a vehicle—it’s a moving clinic staffed by warm, professional teams. Whether in remote towns or bustling suburbs, we park in central spots, invite women in for quick, private examinations, and send every image straight to our radiologists for immediate review. The result? Faster appointments, clearer results, and lives changed because of early action.

Looking Ahead: Scaling for Greater Reach

With proof of concept in hand, WCPI is ready to expand. In the next two years, we aim to deploy mobile units in each of Nigeria’s six geopolitical zones, screening 70,000–100,000 women annually. This scale-up will deepen our data, refine our programs, and create replicable models for governments and grant-makers invested in Women health equity.
