Our Screening Journey: From Clinic to Community

No woman should have to travel miles or pay high fees for a lifesaving checkup. That’s why WCPI’s outreach teams offer mammograms and cervical screenings in villages, markets, schools, and community centers across Nigeria.

We treat every visit as an opportunity—not just to test, but to listen, support, and guide each woman through her health journey. We understand the challenges—long distances, busy schedules, and high costs. So we bring the screening to you—no referral, no stress, no payment needed.
