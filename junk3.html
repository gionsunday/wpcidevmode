Our Integrated Approach

At WCPI, our work is built on four pillars that work together to reduce cancer-related deaths among women in Nigeria— research that informs, education that empowers, screening that detects early, and training that strengthens the health system.

We lead community-based research to understand how women perceive cancer, access care, and respond to screening.
