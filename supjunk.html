Empowering Nigerian Women to Lead Cancer-Free Lives

Through prevention, early detection, education, and support

The Women Cancer Prevention Initiative (WCPI) is a non-profit organization dedicated to the early detection and prevention of breast and cervical cancer among women in Nigeria. We dream of a world where Nigerian women, regardless of age and status, lead beautiful lives free of the burdens of cancer and its consequences.
